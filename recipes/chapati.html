Kenyan Chapati

Description

This Chapati is a deliceous flat bread common to most Kenyan homes that goes well with legumes and beef Stew.It has recently become a street food in Kenya loved by many.

Ingredients

  • 500g All Purpose Flour
  • 3 table spoons of sugar
  • 5 table spoons of vegetable oil
  • 2 cups of butter-nut puree
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Peel and boil a butter-nut till tender then blend them to a perfect consistency.
  2. In a bowl, add 300g of all purpose flour.
  3. Add 3 table spoons of sugar and 1 tsp of salt and mixe thorougly.
  4. In the same bowl, add the blended butter-nut puree and combine the mixture with a spatula.
  5. When the misture is combined, you can proceed kneeding for 10 minutes until the the dough is fully combined and soft
  6. When the misture is combined, you can proceed kneeding for 10 minutes until the the dough is fully combined and soft
  7. Cover your dough with a clean kitchen towel and let to rest for 30 minutes
  8. After 30 minutes, add flour to a flat surface and rool out the dough
  9. Apply vegetable oil on the flat dough and cut to strips them make out balls
  10. Cover the rolled balls for 10 minutes while you heat up the pan
  11. Roll out the balls to a circle and on preffered thickness then place on the hot pan and fip when the sides brown
  12. After both sides have browned up you can a bit of vegetable oil to make the chapati soft and crispy
  13. Once done, store in dish of choice and serve while warm with a hot stew or tea of choice
